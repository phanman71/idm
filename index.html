    About

    So you want to learn more about me eh?

    To put it in shortly, I'm a 3D Artist. I did my undergraduate degree over at Rochester Institute of Technology in 3D digital design.

    After that I got an internship back in my home state of NJ working on a military training game in UE4 where I got to work with some AAA gaming studio veterans and eventually became a full time employee there.

    I then went on to move to Maryland were I got to work on 3D scans of actors, notably Marvel's Infinity Wars Part 1. Using my digital sculpting skills in Zbrush and texture painting in Mari, I cleaned up the data and got it ready to send to the VFX companies or to get 3D printed

    I got the chance to move back to my home state of NJ where I now work at an Army base as a cinematic technical artist where I work on interactive training games, create videos for demostrations/training, use new technologies like mixed reality and more.

    My newest venture is going to graduate school here in NYC while I continue to work my full time job. I'm currenly enrolled at NYU Tandon in the Interactive Digtial Media graduate program where I plan to learn cool new ways of bringing my 3D stuff off the 2D screen.

    reading my blog, you'll be able to follow me on my journey where I learn things like motion capture, augmented reality, virtual reality, mixed reality, 3D printing, and more!
