Motion Capture Week 2

So during the second week of motion capture, we learned how to use Adobe Muse to create a custom avatar and then Mixamo to auto rig it. I decided to make a casual looking fella with regular proportions to avoid risking strange skinning issues later on. I originally wanted to make a custom character but definitely did not have any time this week to do so. Who knew working a full time job and going to grad school full time would be so time consuming haha.

This was also my first time using motion builder. I wanted to try out a stylized character I've been working on in my free time. I knew this was going to be a long shot based on the proportions and stuff but really did not expect these really wacky results! One error I wanted to mention was that I had to rename some of my bones for it to try working.

Well after that experiment I went on to use the carnegie melon motion capture on my guy and exported it to UE4 without too much of an isse. I went with a capture named "playground". I then decided to try and place objects to try and imagine what the invisible playground looked like. I could only imagine that it was some sorta playground with a bunch of bars and steps.

So far so good, I'm pretty excited to try and get a bunch of motion capture data for some future projects and maybe some for my personal ones too!
