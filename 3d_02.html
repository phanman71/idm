3D Art & Aesthetics Week 2 Dirty Mood Board

For my first project of an exterior scene, one of the prompts I recieved was dirty. So for my moodboard, I decieded to try and find the dirtiest, and grossest things I can think of. Bathrooms, sewers, landfills, and slumps kind of all came to mind for me when thinking of nasty things. I tried to think of other things that would envoke thoughts that would make people sick which included slime, excrement, mold and more! I think for something exterior, maybe some sort of environment with a greenish or yellow light spotlighting a misplaced toliet or something covered in gross textures might work out. I don't personally find nature or natural things too dirty so maybe something urban is the kind of setting I'll be putting together.
