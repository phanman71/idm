Creative Coding Week 8

It's been like a decade since I learned electricity stuff back in physics class back in highschool but I did do some soldering and LED stuff not too long ago. This week I got a chance to review the electricity topic as well as setup a LED setup on a bread board for the first time.

For my creative portion, I had a 3D printed boat that I wrapped in tin foil, I thought it'd be fun if a boat would sail onto the water made of foil to make a light house light up. When the boat comes to shore (not tin foil) the light would turn off. It was a nice change of pace to take a break from coding after the crazy mid term wave.
