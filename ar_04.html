AR Week 4 Face Tracking

For this week, we learned SparkAR from Facebook. The first one I made was inspired by ghost in the shell, I took a mesh of an anatomy head from zbrush, threw on a chrome shader and set it up to open up when I opened my mouth. Spark has this cool function where it'll take kind of a screen grab of your current face that you can use as a texture, using this, I had it animate off to reveal the robot head. Luckily Spark has some pretty useful node based programming.
